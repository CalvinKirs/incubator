Apache ShardingSphere (Incubating)

Sharding-Sphere is an ecosystem of transparent distributed database middleware, focusing on data sharding, distributed transaction and database orchestration.

  • Resources: shardingsphere

  • Started: 2018-11-10; Last Status Update: 2020-02-17

  • Reporting: March, June, September, December

  • Committers: 24

  • PPMC members: 14
