Apache Zipkin (Incubating)

Zipkin is a distributed tracing system. It helps gather timing data needed to troubleshoot latency problems in microservice architectures.

  • Resources: zipkin

  • Started: 2018-08-29; Last Status Update: 2019-06-05

  • Reporting: March, June, September, December

  • Committers: 18

  • PPMC members: 17

  • Mentors: Michael Semb Wever (mck), John D. Ament (johndament), Willem Ning Jiang (ningjiang), Andriy Redko (reta), Sheng Wu (wusheng)
