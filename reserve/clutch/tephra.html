Apache Tephra (Incubating)

Tephra is a system for providing globally consistent transactions on top of Apache HBase and other storage engines.

  • Resources: tephra

  • Started: 2016-03-07; Last Status Update: 2016-11-17

  • Reporting: March, June, September, December

  • Committers: 8

  • All Committers are PPMC members
