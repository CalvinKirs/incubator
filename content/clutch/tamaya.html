Apache Tamaya (Incubating)

Tamaya is a highly flexible configuration solution based on an modular, extensible and injectable key/value based design, which should provide a minimal but extendible modern and functional API leveraging SE, ME and EE environments.

  • Resources: tamaya

  • Started: 2014-11-14; Last Status Update: 2015-08-31

  • Reporting: February, May, August, November

  • Committers: 12

  • PPMC members: 10

  • Mentors: John D. Ament (johndament), David Blevins (dblevins), Kanchana Pradeepika Welagedara (kanchana)
